About

I am Kris Faultner, and I have always enjoyed capturing those cherished moments on camera for people to enjoy for years. I especially enjoy photographing families in their true environment as they have fun together. I created Memories Forever Cherished as a way to take my hobby to the next level. I photograph weddings, families, seniors, maternity, and newborns. I will also photograph special events such as charity events, birthdays, or anything you would like to have as memories forever cherished.
